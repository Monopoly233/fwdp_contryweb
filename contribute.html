Soviet Union's contribution to mankind

World War II

The Soviet Union made the greatest contribution to defeating Nazi Germany in World War II and hoisted its flag over the Reichstag in Berlin.

Human exploration of the universe

The Soviet Union was also a pioneer in space exploration, launching the first artificial satellite and astronauts. Pictured here is the first astronaut, Yuri Gagarin.

Decolonization

The Soviet Union made great contributions to decolonization around the world, helping sovereign countries regain property plundered by their colonial masters. Pictured is Khrushchev and Nasser, who work together to take back the Suez Canal from Britain and France.

Gender equality

The Soviet Union was also a pioneer in gender equality, because communism itself opposed confining women to housewife labor and advocated the liberation of mankind. The first female astronaut was born in the Soviet Union, the female sniper. The picture shows the first female astronaut.
